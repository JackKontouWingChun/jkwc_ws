About:
Jack Kontou Wing Chun

Wing Chun Kung Fu is a close combat fighting system based on sensitivity, economy of movement & simplicity. As an individual, each student is personally taught to the highest standard they are willing to aspire to by Sifu Jack Kontou.

Sifu Jack Kontou was trained & certified by Grandmaster Victor Kan and successfully opened & run Grandmaster Victor Kan's first authorised branch.

Wing Chun Kung Fu is a very direct, no-nonsense and fast fighting art of great practical value.

When faced with a confrontation, Wing Chun Kung Fu Skills stand between what the assailant plans to do to you & what would ultimately happen.
